One-Trip Containers

In the industry of shipping containers, "one-trip" refers to containers that have been used only once for international shipping and then brought back to the country of origin. These containers are typically in excellent condition with little to no dents with all of the manufactures paint in tact. Our one-trip containers offer a cost-effective solution without compromising on durability and reliability.


Some applications for these containers include but are not limited to:

  • Jobsite storage
  • Transportation
  • Conversion into living spaces
  • Conversion into workshops or offices
  • Pop-up or retail spaces

Cargo Worthy

Cargo Worthy containers are certified and inspected to meet international shipping standards, ensuring they are structurally sound and safe for transporting goods across oceans. These containers have undergone rigorous testing for strength, weather resistance, and compliance with regulations. Ideal for businesses requiring reliable, high-quality containers for import/export operations.


Key features of our Cargo Worthy containers:

  • Certified for international shipping
  • Weatherproof and durable construction
  • Available in various sizes (20ft, 40ft)
  • Perfect for logistics and supply chain needs

Container Heights Available

It's important to choose the right container height based on your specific needs. Standard containers are suitable for most general storage and shipping needs, while high cube containers provide extra vertical space for larger items or increased storage capacity. Consider the dimensions of the items you plan to store or transport, as well as any height restrictions at your delivery or pickup location when making your selection.


We offer the following container heights:

  • Standard Height: 8.5 feet (2.59 meters)
  • High Cube Height: 9.5 feet (2.89 meters)
